What to expect

This game is currently unsigned (as this costs money), so Windows Defender might make you disable or ignore some warnings in order to play it. This game has no viruses, bitcoin miners, etc. and is completely safe to play! Additionally, this playtest is free to play, and should act as a demo of sorts. If you don't want to fill in the QA form or join the Discord, you do not need to; although, it is greatly appreciated if you do!

This is the first time the game has been playtested since June of 2025. The game is quite difficult, and the tutorial was hastily made for this release. I apologize in advance for any confusion stemming from the tutorial.
For additional help setting up multiplayer, understanding deeper mechanics, or other questions feel free to ask me or others in the Discord community!
Future builds will also be uploaded to this site, so save the URL and check back every now and then.

Please fill out the Google Forms link provided on this website after you have played a decent portion of the playtest! You can always resubmit it if you have later comments to add. Additionally, joining the Discord community will allow you to go into more detail about bug reports, questions, and suggestions for future builds of the game.

Reverie was developed single-handedly by a young, up-and-coming game developer since the end of 2019. All of the assets (art, music, etc.) was created and/or edited solely by me (revowt on Discord). Sound effects were found on freesound.org under the CC0 license or CC BY license (attributions in the menu screen of reverie). Reverie is expected to go through a fundraising phase around October/November of 2026 after building a community to back the game. If you'd like to contribute or join the team, feel free to DM me in the Discord!

Thanks for checking out Reverie!
